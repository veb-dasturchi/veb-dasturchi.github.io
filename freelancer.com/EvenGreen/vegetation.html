Succulents

Succulents are a type of plant that store water in their leaves, stems, or roots. They are often used in landscaping and interior design due to their unique shapes and colors.

Ferns

Ferns are a type of non-flowering plant that reproduce via spores. They are often used in shaded areas and can add a lush and tropical feel to gardens and indoor spaces.

Shrubs

Shrubs are woody plants that are smaller than trees and have multiple stems. They are often used in landscaping to provide structure and privacy, and can also provide food and habitat for wildlife.

Annuals

Annuals are plants that complete their life cycle within one growing season. They are often used in gardens and containers for their vibrant colors and ability to provide continuous blooms throughout the season.

Perennials

Perennials are plants that live for multiple growing seasons. They often die back to the ground during the winter and regrow in the spring. They are often used in landscaping for their reliability and ability to add structure and color to gardens.

Trees

Trees are woody plants that typically have a single trunk and grow taller than shrubs. They provide a range of benefits, including shade, habitat for wildlife, and improved air quality. They are often used in landscaping as focal points or to provide structure and height to gardens.
